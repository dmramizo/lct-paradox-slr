# Grey Literature Sources

This file lists grey literature sources that would be searched in a
supplementary review if requested during peer review. The sources
listed here are NOT part of the main systematic review.

Grey literature is cited in the chapter's introduction and discussion
as policy and empirical context.

## Critical minerals

- IEA — Critical Minerals Outlook (annual, since 2021)
- USGS — Mineral Commodity Summaries (annual)
- World Bank — Minerals for Climate Action (2020)
- IRENA — Critical Materials reports
- EU Joint Research Centre — Critical Raw Materials foresight reports
- Geoscience Australia — Critical Minerals Strategy documents

## Trade and environment

- WTO — World Trade Reports relevant to environmental goods
- UNEP — Trade and environment publications
- OECD — Environmental goods and services publications
